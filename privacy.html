Overview

Unicorn Planner ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the app processes, how it is used, and your choices.

Information the app processes

  • Family task and schedule data you enter in the app (e.g., task titles, dates, assignments).
  • Family Controls permissions (Apple Screen Time / Family Controls) to enable parental controls features.
  • Diagnostic information you choose to send to us via email support (e.g., screenshots you attach).

What we do NOT collect

  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No selling of personal data.
  • No tracking across apps and websites for advertising.

How data is stored

Unicorn Planner may store your app data using Apple services (for example iCloud/CloudKit) depending on your device settings. When iCloud is enabled, your data is stored in your iCloud account and synced between your devices.

Family Controls

Unicorn Planner uses Apple’s Family Controls / Screen Time APIs to provide parental control functionality. These features require parent authorization. The app only uses the permissions necessary to enable the controls you configure.

Third-party services

The app relies on Apple platform services (such as iCloud/CloudKit and Screen Time APIs) to function. We do not integrate third-party advertising SDKs.

Children’s privacy

Unicorn Planner is designed for family use. Parents/guardians are responsible for configuring the app and parental controls. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what is needed to provide the app’s functionality.

Data retention

Data you enter remains on your device and/or your iCloud account until you delete it (or delete the app and related iCloud data, where applicable).

Your choices

  • You can delete tasks and schedules inside the app.
  • You can disable iCloud for the app in iOS settings (if applicable).
  • You can stop using Family Controls features by removing permissions in Screen Time settings.
